    About Us

    BMB ACCOUNTING & CONSULTING, is an accounting firm headed by Mr Bilel MECHLIA, an Accountant registered with the Compagnie des Comptables Tunisiens, works in the areas of accounting, and business consulting.

    Accounting :

    Assistance and bookkeeping, Consolidation of accounts, Payroll management, Project study, Implementation of cost accounting, Implementation of management control system, Outsourcing of internal audit.

    Consulting:
